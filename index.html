About FreshSense

What is FreshSense?

FreshSense is an IoT-powered odor detection system designed specifically for mushroom farms. It uses advanced sensors and AI to identify contamination risks before they become visible to the naked eye.

How Does It Work?

Our proprietary sensors detect volatile organic compounds (VOCs) released by contaminating organisms. The AI analyzes patterns and sends alerts to farmers in real-time via mobile app.

Why Smell Matters

Contamination in mushroom farms produces distinct odors 2-3 days before visible growth appears. FreshSense captures these early warning signs, giving farmers time to act.

Who Should Use It?

Perfect for small family operations, medium-sized commercial farms, and large-scale mushroom producers. Any farm concerned with contamination control will benefit from FreshSense.
